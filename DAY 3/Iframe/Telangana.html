Telangana

 

 Madhavaram, is a village in Munagala Taluk of Suryapet district in the state of Telangana in India.
 Madhavaram located at a distance of 18km from Suryapet and 28km from kodad constituency.
 Madhavaram located on National Highway-65, which is earlier named as NH-9. 
Elevation: 391 m
District: Suryapet
ISO 3166 code: IN-TS
